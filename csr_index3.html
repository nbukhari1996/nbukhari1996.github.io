Why We Do This

Our Mission & Strategy

CSR at ESAD is not a side initiative — it is woven into how we recruit, deploy, and support every one of the 20,000+ workers in our care.

“

Our Mission

To deliver workforce and outsourcing solutions that strengthen the Saudi economy while protecting the dignity, safety, and growth of every worker we place. We measure our success not only in contracts fulfilled, but in lives improved — Saudi nationals finding meaningful careers, expatriate workers receiving fair and respectful treatment, and communities across the Kingdom benefiting from our presence.

“

Our Strategic Vision

To be the most socially responsible staffing and outsourcing partner in the Kingdom — one whose CSR commitments are visible in measurable outcomes: Saudization rates, worker safety records, training completions, and community reinvestment — fully aligned with Saudi Vision 2030's call to build human capital and a resilient, diversified economy.
